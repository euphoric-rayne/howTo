Sketch Lining Coloring Shading

Sketch

Drawing materials

  • Pencil
  • Paper
  • Eraser
  • Pen
  • Colored Pencils and/or Copic Markers

Steps

  1. Draw a circle
  2. draw an almond shape horizontally on the circle
  3. draw another circle inside the almond
  4. draw a tinier circle in the middle of the second circle, and color the small circle in.
